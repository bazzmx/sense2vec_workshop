Two Indians killed in Nepal accident

IANS |

Two Indians were killed after a tractor-trolley they were travelling in overturned in Nepal's Parsa district, police said.

The deceased were identified as Mahendra Paswan, 50, and Puja Kumari, 9, of Champaran district in Bihar, the Himalayan Times reported on Monday.

The vehicle with 25 persons overturned on Sunday night. Nine persons were injured in the accident, said Deputy Superintendent of Police Chakra Bahadur Joshi. The passengers were travelling to the Dudheshwar Mahadev Temple in Parsa district.
