Jul 18 2017 : The Economic Times (Kolkata) Cattle Trade Ban Hits Meat Supply to Restaurants The cattle trade ban has hit the restaurant business over the last four months, with supply chain of meat and meat-related foods being severely impacted and prices spiralling upwards, top restauranteurs and traders said.While supplies fell short by about 75%, prices shot up by 30-45%, report Ratna Bhushan & Madhvi Sally.
