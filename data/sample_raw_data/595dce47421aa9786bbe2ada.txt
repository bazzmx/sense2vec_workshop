NEW DELHI: Shares of Bank of Baroda (up 1.65 per cent), SBI (up 1.64 per cent), Lupin (up 1.43 per cent) and ITC (up 1.30 per cent) were among top Nifty gainers in the morning trade on Thursday. Tata Motors was trading 0.75 per cent up at Rs 435.05. The auto major on Wednesday announced that it has slashed the prices of its passenger vehicles up to 12 per cent, following the roll-out of the Goods and Services Tax (GST).On the other hand, Bajaj Auto (down 1.28 per cent), ONGC (down 1.20 per cent), Bharti Infratel (down 0.91 per cent), NTPC (down 0.88 per cent), Hindustan Unilever (down 0.56 per cent), Wipro (down 0.56 per cent) and IndusInd Bank (down 0.54 per cent) were among top losers.Domestic equity markets opened on a flat note on Thursday following mixed global cues.Most of the Asian peers were trading lower in morning trade on Thursday after minutes from the Federal Reserve's last meeting showed a lack of consensus on the future pace of US interest rate increases.The minutes also showed that officials are divided on unemployment figures. Benchmark Nifty50 index was 26 points up at 9,664, while the 30-share Sensex was 124 points up at 31,369 around 10 am (IST).In the Nifty50 pack, 31 stocks were trading in green, while 20 stocks were in red.On BSE, 408 stocks were trading in red, whereas 1,204 stocks were trading in green.
