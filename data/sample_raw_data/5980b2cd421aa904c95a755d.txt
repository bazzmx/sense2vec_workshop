Shriram City Union Finance today reported a 6.6 percent rise in its net profit at Rs 198.86 crore for the first quarter of the current fiscal.

The company's net profit in the corresponding April-June period of 2016-17 was Rs 181.79 crore.

Total income stood at Rs 1,219.21 crore for the quarter ended June of 2017-18 against Rs 1,055 crore earlier, the company said in a regulatory filing.

Stock of the company closed 2.03 percent up at Rs 2,344.05 on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).
