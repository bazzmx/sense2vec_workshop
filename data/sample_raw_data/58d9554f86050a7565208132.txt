G Vijay Kumar, CFO, Kaveri Seed in an interview to CNBC-TV18 said the board has approved buyback of upto 29.62 lakh shares at Rs 675 per share via tender offer route.

The price is at a premium to current market price by about Rs 220 per share.

The total buyback size would be around Rs 200 crore. The cash on books currently stood at Rs 600 crore and post buyback would be around Rs 400 crore.

He said the promoters will be participating in the buyback. Promoters currently hold 54.4 percent.
