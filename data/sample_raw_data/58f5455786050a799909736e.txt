Apr 18 2017 : The Economic Times (Delhi) Cong Cautious Ahead of SC Ruling on Talaq NEW DELHI: OPB With the Supreme Court verdict on the triple talaq expected in less than a month, Congress spokesperson Abhishek Singhvi on Monday said the party “fully stands for gender justice“ but stressed everybody should “wait for SC order“ and not “politicise or sensationalise“ the issue and the proceedings. Singhvi also spoke about how Congress governments --from Nehru-Patel era to Sonia Gandhi-Manmohan Singh UPA era -had taken many steps to ensure gender justice. He made no mention of Rajiv Gandhi government's U-turn on Shah Bano episode.
